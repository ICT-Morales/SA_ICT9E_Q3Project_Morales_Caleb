The life of James Rallison


Image Description

James Rallison is a well-known storytime animator on youtube. He was born on May 14, 1996 in Maricopa Country, Arizona, where he used to live with his mom, Janette Rallison, as well as his sister, Faith. He joined the platform on Aug 30 2014, where he would upload vidoes pertaining to his early life as a person. He would talk about his experience with weird custormers at work, what his life with his family was like, his relationship with his girlfriend, Collabirate with other animators, and QnA tours with friends. Not only does he do animations about his life, he also made animated music videos, like his most popular one, Life is Fun, made by him and musician, Boyinaband, ranging over 169 million views.

Projects


Image of project Description
James Rallison was not only known for his cartoony life animations, he was also known for the creation of his own Neflix show, titled, "ODDBALLS". The show was announced way back in June 23 2022 on James' channel, were he explains how the shows was a struggle to pitch during the pandemic. He even explained in a seperate video called, "pitching a show" were he talks about the constant struggle for ppitching, "ODDBALLS", to the comapnies and how to hook the people who are willing to pitch it. The show was released on october 7, 2022 and it follows James, the creator of the show, and his friends, Max and Echo, on their wacky adventures and learning life lesson in an odd way, hence the title.
James was able to create merchandise for his lovable fans. He made merch like a book version of his life titled,"how to be cool and other things I definitley learned from growing up", plushies of his character from his Neflix show, and AR puzzle games. He also made sweaters, hoddies, keychains, jogging pants, as well as back to school merch like pens, bags, sticker, and lunch bags.
